BeatKcal - Diet Helper

Lead & App Design, 2015.6

Armed with previous experiences, I challenged myself
by participating in an APP design competition and won the Best Design award.

Competing with mature teams with startup experience,
my team was required to complete the entire process
from idea creation to product implementation in one month.

function

Detecting or estimating the weight of food
to control daily calorie was extremely difficult
because of my own weight loss experience.

Hence, I proposed to obtain a fat loss assistant
connected to a portable Bluetooth scale to record calorie intake
and the Apple watch to record calorie consumption as our product.

design

After recognizing that superior design logic should reduce unnecessary interaction,
I attempted to incorporate this idea into my application.
For example,
the application would recommend detailed diet menu according to the calorie plan to users
as soon as they record their meal.

development

Apart from completing market research and UI design independently,
I developed interaction animation and front-end part with objective-c language.

This experience allowed me to enjoy the sense of creating a practical application that can turn complex steps into a simple behavior through HCI.
