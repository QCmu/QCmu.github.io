During my internship at Nielsen Norman Group, I worked on user experience research on international e-commerce websites. Mentored by Jakob Nielsen, I treasured the experience to work as a real UX researcher.

My research is a part of the revisement of the previous e-commerce research at Nielsen Norman Group. During the research, I conducted more than 30 sessions of usability testing (lab setting) on 50 international e-commerce websites in different perspectives of user research.
